Our story

Your Neighborhood Handyman

To provide Irving homeowners with reliable, high-quality handyman services that make home maintenance stress-free.

Clean Magic TX, LLC. was founded on the belief that every home deserves care and attention. Starting with a small toolkit and a commitment to excellence, we built a reputation for honesty, skill, and punctuality. Over the years, we've helped hundreds of Irving residents with everything from leaky faucets to full home renovations.

Today, we continue to grow by staying true to our roots: showing up on time, communicating clearly, and delivering work that we're proud to put our name on. Whether it's a small repair or a major project, we treat every home like our own.
